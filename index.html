What is Direct Swap Connections?

Direct Swap Connections is a student-built platform that makes BU's room swap process actually work. Instead of posting in group chats and hoping for the best, you can browse a searchable database of students who want to swap — filter by building, room type, occupancy, and gender housing — and reach out directly.

It's free, fast, and built by BU students for BU students.

How It Works

1
Sign in with your @bu.edu Google account to submit or view contact info.
2
Submit your listing — describe your current room and what you're looking for.
3
Browse listings — filter by building, room type, gender housing, and more.
4
Reach out directly via email, Reddit, phone, or whatever contact they listed.
5
Complete the swap in StarRez — both students must initiate the swap there officially.
